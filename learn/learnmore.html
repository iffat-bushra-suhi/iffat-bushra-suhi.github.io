More About AniArt Today

This is a community who specializes on a certain type of art, the anime/manga one! That means that the people who draw anime/manga or just like looking at that type of art are very welcome here! In this community you can find and interact with various artist and exchange tips to improve your art! You can rate art and have your art rated/reviewed by others! You can make blog, polls and you can also design your profile! You can take part on weekly challenges and get certain rewards for doing so! AniArt Today is an online art communication service that connects artists with other artists and enthusiasts. It is a place where you can enjoy submitting and browsing countless artworks. Aside from the wide variety of illustrations submitted on AniArt Today, there are frequently held illustration projects from both users and our staff. Users can collaborate with each other and create new works by utilizing this co-creation platform. I hope you all have the best time on the AniArt Today!
