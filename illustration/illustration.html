ILLUSTRATION

AniArt Today is a place to express yourself, discover yourself, and bond over the stuff you love. It's where your interests connect you with your people.With anime expanding and taking over the world, many are inspired by some great illustrators. Taking pen and pencil, the next generation of artists are using AniArt Today to promote their art. From drawings to water colors, 3-D art and even food, these artists are killing the anime scene with their own interpretations of classic characters and their own creations as well. Maybe one day they will be the next Naruto or One Piece? Either way AniArt Today has been a great tool to see new talent rising!
